What Is Domain Name

A domain name is the unique address of your website on the internet. It's what people type into their browsers to find and visit your site. For example, in the domain name www.checkwithdeveloper.com, "checkwithdeveloper.com" is the domain name. It serves as a human-readable way to locate and access a specific website on the vast network of the internet.
